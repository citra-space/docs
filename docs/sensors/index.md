# Sensors

Sensors are the observation equipment used for satellite tracking and data collection. In the Citra Space platform, sensors represent the telescopes and antennas deployed at ground stations that perform the actual observations.

From here you can:

- **Browse and filter** all sensors across your ground stations
- **Configure telescopes** with optical specifications and scheduling parameters
- **Configure antennas** with radio frequency parameters and scheduling settings
- **Manage tasking schedules** to schedule, fulfill, and cancel observation tasks
- **Work with observation data** including image uploads for telescopes and RF captures for antennas
