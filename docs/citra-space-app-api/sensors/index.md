# Sensors

Sensors are the observation equipment used for satellite tracking and data collection. In the Citra Space platform, sensors represent the telescopes and antennas deployed at ground stations that perform the actual observations.

From here you can:

- [**Browse and filter**](browsing-and-filtering) all sensors across your ground stations
- [**Configure telescopes**](telescopes/) with optical specifications and scheduling parameters
- [**Configure antennas**](antennas/) with radio frequency parameters and scheduling settings
- Manage tasking schedules for [**telescopes**](telescopes/tasking-schedule) and [**antennas**](antennas/tasking-schedule) — schedule, fulfill, and cancel observation tasks
- Review [**image uploads**](telescopes/image-uploads) for telescopes and [**RF captures**](antennas/rf-captures) for antennas
