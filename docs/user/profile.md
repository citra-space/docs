# Profile

The Profile page provides a summary of your activity and equipment on the Citra Space platform, including your ground stations and sensors.

## Understanding Your Profile

Your profile aggregates key statistics about your account and provides quick access to the ground stations and sensors you own. This page is useful for reviewing your contributions and navigating directly to your equipment.

## Profile Statistics

The top of the profile page displays the following summary statistics:

| Statistic | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Member Duration** | How long you have been a member, calculated from your account creation date |
| **RF Captures** | Total number of RF captures recorded by your antennas |
| **Image Uploads** | Total number of images uploaded from your telescopes |

## Ground Stations

The Ground Stations section displays a grid of all ground stations you own. The grid uses the same columns as the main [Ground Stations](../ground-stations/) list:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name** | Ground station name (click to navigate to the detail page) |
| **Latitude** | Geographic latitude in decimal degrees |
| **Longitude** | Geographic longitude in decimal degrees |
| **Altitude** | Elevation above sea level in meters |

## Sensors

The Sensors section displays a grid of all telescopes and antennas you own. The grid uses the same columns as the main [Sensors](../sensors/) list:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name** | Sensor name (click to navigate to the detail page) |
| **Type** | Telescope or Antenna |
| **Ground Station** | The ground station the sensor is assigned to |

## Public Profile

Your public profile is accessible at `/users/{username}` and displays your profile statistics and equipment to other Citra Space users. Select the link on your profile page to view your public profile.

## Use Cases

The profile page is useful for:

- **Monitoring activity** — Tracking your RF capture and image upload counts over time
- **Managing equipment** — Quickly navigating to your ground stations and sensors
- **Sharing your work** — Directing collaborators to your public profile page
