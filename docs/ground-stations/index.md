# Ground Stations

Ground stations define observer locations on Earth's surface for satellite tracking and observation. In the Citra Space platform, each ground station represents a physical site with geographic coordinates and optional sensor equipment.

From here you can:

- **Browse and manage** your ground station catalog
- **Calculate satellite passes** to determine when objects are visible from a location
- **Configure sensors** such as telescopes and antennas at each site
- **Monitor weather conditions** to support observation planning
